About Us

Company Profile


JAEE is a professional manufacturer and exporter that is concerned with design,development and production of faucet, mixer and shower. We are located in Shuikou Town, Guangdong province, which is known of "Kingdom of Chinese sanitary ware".

All of faucet and shower comply with international quality standards and are greatly appreciated in a variety of different markets throughout the world. JAEE covers an area of 4,000 sq.m, and the annual production capacity is more than 200,000 pcs. Our well-equipped facilities and excellent quality control throughout all stages of production enables us to guarantee total customer satisfaction. As a result of our high quality faucet and outstanding customer service, we have gained a global sales network reaching France, US, Germany, Brazil, South African,Saudi Arabia etc.

If you are interested in any of our faucet and shower or would like to discuss an OEM/ODM service, please feel free to contact us. We are looking forward to forming successful business relationships with new clients around the world in the near future.
